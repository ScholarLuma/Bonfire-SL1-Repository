The Bonfire Minstat Guidelines

~ Complete and detailed ruleset for Minstat runs ~

Notice: We don't care about the rules in other communities.

When Fromsoftware players talk about a 'minstat run' they are discussing runs in which the player starts the game with the minstat equivalent class then complete the game without leveling up. For Souls this is SL1, for Bloodborne it's BL4 and for Elden Ring it's RL1.

These runs have a long history in the community, with a multitude of challenges created from them. In this page you can read general info on Minstat Runs, the proof you need to submit in the Bonfire Discord and banned strategies for Legendary SL1 challenges.

The base for this repository was provided by Sen#0126 over on the Demon's Souls Discord. This page exists only because of the hard work of Sen and the moderation team over there so please check them out!

  • Champion Submissions

    • Each game has it's own unique criteria regarding Champion run proof, please refer to the individual games section to understand what you will need when submitting proof for your run!

  • Legend Submissions

    • For Legend runs you will need to record all individual boss fights from before you enter the fog gate to when the boss dies. Each game and run may have separate requirements, please refer to the individual games section to understand what you will need to include when submitting proof for your run!

  • Submitting your run

    • When submitting your Champion run to be checked we usually require screenshots of your stats and boss souls, bonfires or boss arenas. It can vary depending on the game. For Legend runs we highly recommend you upload them all individually into one single playlist on YouTube, that way whoever is checking the run can verify without having to waste time searching for the fights; however some runs may require the entirety of the run to be recorded. When you believe you have all of the necessary proof send Luma#0246 a message.

  • All bosses must be slain!

    • Every boss listed must be slain. A boss can be defined by the sight of a health bar and/or a fog gate (regardless of the need to enter through such) but exceptions may be made. All required bosses for each individual game are listed in their dedicated section.

    Make sure to fully read the section you're interested in doing before starting your run!

  • No exploits will be tolerated

    • There's multiple cheese tactics or exploits in the Soulsborne games. Any level skips, glitches that break boss AI or glitches that change the fight to what it's not intended as are not tolerated. Exceptions may be made on a case-by-case basis. It comes down to the discretion of the person checking your run. None are allowed. By cheating you are undervaluing yourself and others.

    Important! In terms of "glitches", it is generally allowed to dupe or spawn in items as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character. Items may also be spawned in if the runner missed the opportunity to obtain them and can no longer do so.

  • No help allowed.

    • Killing bosses via summoning players or with the help of NPCs is not acceptable. Summoning players to help you progress through the game is also not allowed. Every run found breaching this rule will be disqualified.

    Important! In terms of trading, it is generally allowed to get items dropped as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character. Items may also be traded to you if the runner accidentally missed the opportunity to obtain them and can no longer do so, usually because of questlines.

  • No cheating.

    • Multiple "interesting tactics" have been already been tried and subsequently cut down. The entire point of a challenge run is to challenge yourself. These directly combat that. Each run will be looked at closely, so don't try anything.

    It is not allowed to obtain items before it is usually possible. No debug. No scripts nor external tools.

    - Turning on iframes
    - Script-killing bosses via flags
    - Messing with stats
    - Cheesing the AI...


    ... and so on will result in a disqualification of the run in question, with possible further moderation action to be taken.

As previously said, there is no specific max number of firebombs, but this paragraph should help runners understand that it must not be high.
